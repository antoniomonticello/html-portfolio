The Best Movies According to Tony

My Top Three Movies of All Time

Need for Speed

This is a very thrilling, action packed movie. There are a ton of cool cars, exhilarating races, and emotional moments that even the viewer will be struck to the heart with.

Deadpool

This is by far one of the funniest movies I have seen in a long time. There are fight scenes, comedic scenes, and some gore scenes. The role is perfectly played by Ryan Reynolds nd could not picture it any other way.

After (2019)

This movie is part of a romance series on netflix. The main character Hardin is a not so caring tough guy in the start. He soon realizes after a bet he is falling in love with the co star and is thrown into a whirlwind of emotions. The movie displays so many different emotion the viewer can feel and truly left me wanting more.
